### **Donate to Tucson Food Share**
{{% /center %}}

We love **recurring monetary donations** and no amount is too small! Here's how to make a one-time or recurring donation:

- We accept donations via credit card or PayPal through our [Ko-Fi account](https://ko-fi.com/tucsonfoodshare)
- We accept direct donations to our [**PayPal**](https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr?cmd=_s-xclick&hosted_button_id=WUUF53KRC93X6&source=url). The listed name of "Root + Branch" is our fiscal sponsor that provides nonprofit status. You can read more about them [here](https://healthoutreach.org/)

For **one-time donations**, you can use the above options or we also support the following methods:

- [**Venmo**](https://venmo.com/FNBtucson) is great for one-time donations as no fee is deducted. The last four digits of the phone number are 4088.
- You can pay us with Zelle! We are registered under tucsonfoodshare@gmail.com.
- Cash or checks accepted in-person or by mail at **2500 N Stone Ave, Tucson AZ 85705.** Make checks out to **Root + Branch** (our fiscal sponsor), with Tucson Food Share in the memo line.

To donate food or other resources, feel free to stop by during any of our grocery sharing hours and drop them off. For larger quantities that might require other help or coordination, email [tucsonfoodshare@gmail.com](mailto:tucsonfoodshare@gmail.com) or call [520-222-9242](tel:5202229242)

[**Click here for our wishlist of supplies!**](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1JfN48LswvZb5TzC5nPy5j3iY430SpQJRiiTH6txS4MU/edit?usp=sharing)

Donations are tax deductible; email us if you need a receipt.

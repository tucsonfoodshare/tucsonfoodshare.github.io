Donate to Tucson Food Share / Dona a Tucson Food Share (Español abajo)

We love recurring donations and no amount is too small! Here's how to make a one-time or recurring donation:

  • We accept donations via credit card or PayPal through our Ko-Fi account

  • We accept direct donations to our PayPal. The listed name of "Root + Branch" is our fiscal sponsor that provides nonprofit status. You can read more about them here

For one-time donations, you can use the above options or we also support the following methods:

  • Venmo is great for one-time donations as no fee is deducted. The last four digits of the phone number are 4088.

  • Cash or checks accepted in-person or by mail at 2500 N Stone Ave, Tucson AZ 85705. Make checks out to Root + Branch (our fiscal sponsor), with Tucson Food Share in the memo line.

To donate food or other resources, email tucsonfoodshare@gmail.com or call 520-222-9242
